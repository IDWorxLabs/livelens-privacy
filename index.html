Privacy Policy – LiveLens

Effective Date: March 2, 2026

LiveLens is developed and operated by INTEL DESIGN WORX ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the LiveLens mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When creating an account, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

This information is used for authentication, security, subscription management, and account functionality.

1.2 Video Content

LiveLens allows users to record and save videos within the app library. Videos manually recorded and saved remain stored within the app. Live streaming sessions and screen share sessions are not recorded or stored.

1.3 AI Conversations

AI interactions are processed temporarily and discarded after processing. Conversation history is not permanently stored.

1.4 Location Data

Location access is optional and can be enabled or disabled at any time. Location data is used only to enhance contextual assistance.

1.5 Device & Usage Data

We may collect limited technical data such as device type, operating system version, crash logs, and app performance data to improve service stability and security.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide AI assistance
  • Enable video and live features
  • Manage subscriptions
  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Prevent fraud and misuse

3. Subscriptions & Payments

LiveLens offers subscription-based services processed securely via Google Play Billing. We do not store credit card or payment details.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no digital system is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

LiveLens is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Third-Party Services

LiveLens may use third-party services for AI processing, analytics, and payment processing. These providers operate under their own privacy policies.

7. User Rights

Users may update their information, delete their account, disable location access, and cancel subscriptions at any time.

8. Data Retention

Account data is retained while the account is active. Saved videos remain stored until manually deleted by the user. AI conversations are discarded after processing.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
